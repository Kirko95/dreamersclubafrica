What is Dreamers Club Africa


Dreamers Club Africa is a fun club whose vision is to empower the next generation to be smart money managers, build and grow wealth. Our goal is to increase the financial literacy of the next generation of future entrepreneurs. We offer money management, investments and entrepreneurship education for kids and teens to equip them with the essential knowledge and life skills for the 21st century. Our methodology simulates the real world and is based on interactive fun learning, debate, action and reflection with emphasis on social responsibility. Our programs are aligned with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) Dreamers Club Africa was founded in 2018 by Andrew Kibe. Listen to him talk about Dreamers Club Africa.

Money management

Money management is a life skill because it is a commodity used for life. You must gain control over your money or the lack of it will forever control you. The myth that teaching children about money is not ‘good’ leads to the later. Evidence is out in the open with most adults struggling with personal finance. Like other skills fostered from a tender age such as music, football, etc money management should not be expected..

Power of The Mind

Before you can transform your wallet from poor to rich, you have to transform your mind from poor to rich. The size of your success is measured by the strength of your desire; the size of your dream; and how you handle disappointments along the way. Failure defeats losers. Failure inspires winners. These and more are at the center of the mind – subconscious and unconscious. Understanding the difference and the role each plays on our day to day isextremely important to have a proper mindset going forward. Financial freedom is a mental, emotional and educational process.

Investments

Investments are about making money work for you and not vice versa. The secret to real financial freedom or the way to wealth is through cash flowing assets. The philosophy of the rich and the poor is this: the rich invest their money and spend what is left. The poor spend their money and invest what is left.

Entrepreneurship

We cannot solve our problems with the same thinking we used when we created them. Inside every problem lies an opportunity. Therefore, innovation is at the center of true entrepreneurship. Most people never get wealthy simply because they are not trained to recognize opportunities right in front of them.

Why Dreamers Club Africa

Dreamers Club Africa is a believer in information based on data and therefore research is at the heart of our DNA. Before the formation of the club, we conducted a small research to understand the level of financial literacy in Kenya. The data collected was to be our starting point, which can be measured and referenced to. We interviewed retirees who had prime jobs during their working days and the working nation.

Over 95% of these retirees heavily rely on pension and up keep from their children for survival. The businesses ventures they set up after retirement were not successful. They observe lack of money management, investment and entrepreneurship knowledge and skills as the root cause to this. However, their greatest concern was that the working nation was heading in the same direction but when warned or advised, they don’t listen.

As for the working nation, we simply asked two questions. The first was “if you had the financial knowledge you have today before you started working, how different do you think your life would have been today?” 100% of the respondents were of the view that their lives would have been far much better financially. The second question was “Given the knowledge you have acquired so far, what have you done or are you doing to ensure your children gain that knowledge before they start working?” Over 92% had either never thought of it this way or had done nothing.

Most parents/caregiver already know how important it is to teach their kids about money and how to manage it properly. But there’s a difference between knowing and taking action. The question then became, why or what stops them from taking action? The answer to this question lies in the psychology of understanding the difference between the conscious and the subconscious mind and how they operate. It is this understanding that differentiates Dreamers Club Africa. .

Reasons To Join:

  1. PARENTS WISH THEY HAD THIS FINANCIAL HEAD START
    Imagine if you had been taught at a tender age about money and, more specifically, that the way to wealth is through cash flowing assets. How different would your life be today if you had learned these concepts at an early age?
  2. THE BUCK STOPS WITH THE PARENT
    Today’s parents need to understand that psychologically, financial literacy foundation is developed as early as age 3, mainly through a child observing his/her surrounding. A parent who does not take charge to ensure the right behavior is developed, simply hands over that responsibility to other powerful forces such as media adverts to do that work for them. These adverts instill ‘buy’ habits subconsciously. It has always been, will always be the parent’s/caregiver’s decision, knowingly or unknowingly, what the foundation will be. Click here to learn more on this.
  3. DEVELOPING THE RIGHT FINANCIAL MIND SET
    Kids are like sponges. From the time they are babies, they absorb what they see and hear around them. They get their ideas about money from their environment and this solidifies to a habit. Without the right mind set at tender years, they will struggle as adults to break from the solidified wrong mindset - psychologically.
  4. THEY LEARN ‘HOW’ TO THINK, NOT ‘WHAT’ TO THINK
    When we teach kids “A” is for apple, in effect, we converge their thinking and creativity. This is teaching them, ‘what’ to think. If we ask them to come up with names that start with “A”, in effect, we diverge their thinking and creativity. This is teaching them ‘how’ to think. Our programs are designed to teach ‘how’ to think.
  5. SIMULATING REAL LIFE EXAMPLES
    Our programs are hands-on learning, practical and engaging activities that simulate the real-life financial dilemmas. This includes - savings, needs & wants, good & bad credit, assets, liabilities, cash-flow, generating unique business ideas through problem solving, investing in different financial products etc. We make it very practical.
  6. BREAKING DOWN OF TECHNICAL TERMS
    Terms such as assets, liabilities, cash flow, credit score & reports, are broken down to their level of understanding with ease of understanding and applying. Kids & teens just want to have fun. We make learning technical terms fun and entertaining so that those lessons stick in their minds.
  7. THEY LEARN THE SECRET OF THE WEALTHY
    Before joining the programs, kids & teens normally have a mindset that as adults they will need to work for money. During their graduation, that is having attained the 3 levels, they will have a mindset that as adults, money should work for them. That is – the way to wealth is through cash flowing assets
